ALL ABOUT VERVE

VERVE VIT is an exhilarating intercollegiate festival hosted by VIT, Mumbai, bringing together students from diverse academic backgrounds to showcase their talents and celebrate creativity.

With a vibrant mix of cultural events VERVE VIT offers a platform for participants to engage in friendly competition, fostering a spirit of collaboration and camaraderie. The festival encompasses a wide array of activities, including music performances, dance competitions, Mono acts, street plays and much more. Known for its energetic atmosphere and innovative events, VERVE VIT has become a highlight on the academic calendar, attracting participants and spectators alike, creating lasting memories and fostering a sense of community within the VIT, Mumbai campus.
